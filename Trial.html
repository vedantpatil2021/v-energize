Enerain

The new tech, droplet-based electricity generator, uses dielectric film to store charges when water droplets hit the surface & eliminates the surface effect, which dramatically improves efficiency.The design of the energy generator includes two electrodes - one made up of aluminium and other made up of an indium tin oxide (ITO) electrode with a film of PTFE deposited on it, which is responsible for the charge generations ,storage , and induction.
When the spreading water connects the two electrodes, all the stored charges on the PTFE can be released for generation of electric current.This system works by gathering Kinetic energy from the raindrops which can be regarded as free and renewable.This system can handle extreme rainfall, the charge will keep on increasing till a saturation point.
